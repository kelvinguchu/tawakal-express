About Tawakal Express

Our Journey

Starting as a physical money transfer company in Somalia, we've evolved to simplify the entire process through innovative delivery methods that connect families across continents.

After serving thousands of families through conventional methods, our development as a premier international money transfer company stems from embracing new technologies. To serve our customers more efficiently and securely, we've developed a suite of digital services including E-Tawakal, Banana Pay, and T-Plus Mobile Money Transfer.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to make our customers' lives a whole lot brighter.
